Community Bulletin

The local community is hosting a series of exciting events this summer. From tech meetups to music festivals, there is something for everyone. The Tech Innovators Meetup will be held on June 10th in New York City. The AI and Machine Learning Conference is scheduled for May 15th in Chicago. The Frontend Development Bootcamp runs from July 1st to 3rd in Los Angeles. Community members are encouraged to register early as seats are limited. All events are open to residents of all ages and backgrounds. Volunteer opportunities are also available for those interested. Contact the community center for more information and registration details. We look forward to seeing everyone at these wonderful events this season.
